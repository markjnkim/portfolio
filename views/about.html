+ Road to Web Development

Currently as a TA for a full stack web development class, I am blessed to be in a learning environment while utilizing my skills at explaining complex concepts in basic terms and giving back to the community, teaching skills that can change careers and lives.

As the company founder at Fuzebox, my successful track record can be attributed to a laser focus on the customer. Whether it was creating the perfect environment that exuded excitement and energy, finding the right trend at the right time, training the staff in client success, or implementing an inventory management system to analyze buy trends; in the end it was always the customers' perspective that made all the decisions.
